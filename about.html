About Us

Learn more about Daily News and our commitment to quality journalism.

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Daily News has grown from a small local newspaper to one of the most trusted sources of news and information across the country. For over 30 years, we have remained committed to our founding principles of accuracy, independence, and integrity in journalism.

What began as a team of five passionate journalists has evolved into a diverse newsroom of over 200 reporters, editors, photographers, and digital media specialists working across multiple platforms to deliver timely and relevant news to our readers.

Throughout our history, we have adapted to the changing media landscape while maintaining our core values. From print to digital, we continue to innovate in how we deliver news while upholding the highest standards of journalistic excellence.

Our Mission

"To provide accurate, unbiased, and timely news that informs, educates, and empowers our readers to make better decisions and engage more deeply with the world around them."

Integrity

We adhere to the highest ethical standards in our reporting, ensuring accuracy, fairness, and transparency in all our work.

Truth

We are committed to pursuing the truth through rigorous research, fact-checking, and in-depth investigation.

Independence

We maintain editorial independence, free from political pressure or commercial influence.

Diversity

We value diverse perspectives and strive to represent all communities in our coverage and our newsroom.
