Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a news tip?

You can submit news tips by emailing tips@dailynews.com or by using our contact form above. Please include as much detail as possible, and any relevant photos or documents.

How do I report an error in an article?

We strive for accuracy in all our reporting. If you find an error, please email corrections@dailynews.com with the article title, the error, and the correct information.

How can I advertise with Daily News?

For advertising inquiries, please contact our advertising department at advertising@dailynews.com or call +91 (555) 765-4321. We offer print, digital, and integrated advertising solutions.

Do you accept guest articles or opinion pieces?

Yes, we welcome guest contributions and opinion pieces. Please send your pitch or completed article to editorial@dailynews.com with "Guest Submission" in the subject line.
