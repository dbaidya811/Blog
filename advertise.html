Frequently Asked Questions

What are your advertising rates?

Our advertising rates vary based on placement, duration, and format. We offer flexible packages to suit different budgets and marketing objectives. Please contact our advertising team for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

How do you measure campaign performance?

We provide comprehensive analytics for all advertising campaigns, including impressions, clicks, engagement rates, time spent, and conversion metrics. For sponsored content, we also track social shares, comments, and overall reach. Our advertisers receive detailed reports throughout their campaign.

What is the minimum campaign duration?

Our standard minimum campaign duration is one month for most advertising formats. However, we offer shorter durations for specific high-impact placements like homepage takeovers or newsletter sponsorships. We can discuss your timeline requirements during our initial consultation.

Do you offer targeting options?

Yes, we offer various targeting options including category/section targeting, geographic targeting, device targeting, and demographic targeting based on our user data. This ensures your message reaches the most relevant audience for your brand.
